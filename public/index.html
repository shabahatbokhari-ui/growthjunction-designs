How to use these. Open each in a browser. Spend 30 seconds, not 30 minutes. The first one that makes you say "yes, that is us" is the direction. Hybrids welcome. We can lift the hero of one onto the structure of another. The point of seven contrasting directions is to surface preference, not to ship any of these as-is. All mockups are wireframe-fidelity: placeholder photography, illustrative copy, sample case studies. Not production designs.
