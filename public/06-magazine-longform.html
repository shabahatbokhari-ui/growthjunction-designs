Strategy is the easy part. The humans are the work.

A note on why we started Growth Junction, what we have learned in twenty years inside boardrooms, and the kettle we keep on for whoever needs a real conversation.

A short introduction

We are a small advisory practice. We do the work that lives between the strategy deck and the all-hands meeting. The conversations the executives cannot have with each other, the structures that need to bend for the strategy to breathe, the well-being foundation without which none of the rest survives a hard quarter.

We have spent twenty years in the rooms where these decisions get made, inside and outside the company. We know the difference between a deck that survives the rollout and one that does not. We will tell you, plainly, which one you are holding.

If your strategy depends on the people executing it, the work is human before it is anything else.

One-on-one and team coaching for chief executives and the leaders they are betting on. Marshall Goldsmith stakeholder-centered method. The work is confidential, unhurried, and judged on what changes in the room, not what is written on the slide.
