A note from our founder

Strategy is the easy part. The humans are the work.

I have spent twenty years inside boardrooms and on the other side of the table. I have scaled organisations through their hardest seasons. I have sat with leaders the day after the layoff and the morning after the merger. I have learned that almost everything that matters in business sits below the slide deck.

I built Growth Junction because the work I cared about most kept being treated as soft, when in fact it is the hardest thing in the building. We are deliberately small. We work with leaders who want a real conversation more than they want a framework. We treat well-being not as a perk, but as the foundation that lets the rest of the work survive the year.

If something in this matches what you are sitting with, the kettle is on.
